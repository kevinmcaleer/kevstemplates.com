# Project Management Certifications

Professional certifications validate your knowledge and demonstrate commitment to the profession. This guide helps you understand the options and choose the right path for your career.

---

## Why Get Certified?

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Credibility** | Demonstrates verified knowledge to employers |
| **Career advancement** | Often required for senior roles |
| **Higher salary** | Certified PMs typically earn 20%+ more |
| **Structured learning** | Forces comprehensive study |
| **Network** | Join professional communities |
| **Global recognition** | Credentials recognised worldwide |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

---

## Certification Comparison

| Certification | Issuing Body | Best For | Validity |
|---------------|--------------|----------|----------|
| **PMP** | PMI | Experienced PMs, global recognition | 3 years |
| **PRINCE2** | Axelos/PeopleCert | UK/Europe, structured environments | No expiry* |
| **AgilePM** | APMG | Agile practitioners | 3 years |
| **CSM/PSM** | Scrum Alliance/Scrum.org | Scrum teams | 2 years / No expiry |
| **CAPM** | PMI | Entry-level PMs | 3 years |
| **APM PMQ** | APM | UK-based PMs | No expiry |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

*PRINCE2 doesn't expire but many employers prefer recent certification

## PMP (Project Management Professional)

### Overview
The **PMP** from the Project Management Institute (PMI) is the most widely recognised PM certification globally.

### Requirements

| Requirement | With Degree | Without Degree |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Education** | 4-year degree | High school/secondary |
| **PM Experience** | 36 months | 60 months |
| **PM Education** | 35 hours | 35 hours |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Exam Details

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Format** | 180 questions, multiple choice & other formats |
| **Duration** | 230 minutes |
| **Passing score** | Not published (scaled scoring) |
| **Cost** | $555 USD (PMI member) / $405 USD (member) |
| **Delivery** | Online proctored or test centre |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Content Domains

The PMP exam covers three domains:

| Domain | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| **People** | 42% |
| **Process** | 50% |
| **Business Environment** | 8% |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Maintaining Certification
- Earn 60 PDUs (Professional Development Units) every 3 years
- Pay renewal fee
- Adhere to PMI Code of Ethics

**Learn more:** [PMI Website](https://www.pmi.org/certifications/project-management-pmp)

## PRINCE2

### Overview
**PRINCE2** (PRojects IN Controlled Environments) is widely used in the UK, Europe, and Australia. It provides a structured methodology rather than a body of knowledge.

### Certification Levels

| Level | Target Audience | Prerequisites |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Foundation** | Anyone starting with PRINCE2 | None |
| **Practitioner** | Those applying PRINCE2 | Foundation |
| **Agile Foundation** | Combining PRINCE2 with Agile | None |
| **Agile Practitioner** | Advanced Agile integration | Agile Foundation or PRINCE2 Practitioner |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Foundation Exam

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Format** | 60 multiple choice questions |
| **Duration** | 60 minutes |
| **Passing score** | 55% (33/60) |
| **Open book** | No |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Practitioner Exam

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Format** | 68 questions based on scenarios |
| **Duration** | 150 minutes |
| **Passing score** | 55% (38/68) |
| **Open book** | Yes (official manual only) |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Key Topics
- 7 Principles
- 7 Themes
- 7 Processes
- Tailoring PRINCE2 to the project

**Learn more:** [Axelos PRINCE2](https://www.axelos.com/certifications/prince2)

## Agile Certifications

### Certified ScrumMaster (CSM)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Issuing body** | Scrum Alliance |
| **Requirements** | Attend 2-day course with Certified Scrum Trainer |
| **Exam** | 50 questions, 60 minutes, 74% pass |
| **Renewal** | Every 2 years, 20 SEUs + fee |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Professional Scrum Master (PSM)

| Level | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **PSM I** | 80 questions, 60 minutes, 85% pass, $150 |
| **PSM II** | 30 questions, 90 minutes, 85% pass, $250 |
| **PSM III** | Essay + multiple choice, 120 minutes, $500 |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

**Key difference:** CSM requires training; PSM can be self-study.

### AgilePM

| Level | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Foundation** | 50 questions, 40 minutes, 50% pass |
| **Practitioner** | 4 questions, 150 minutes, 50% pass |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

Based on DSDM Agile Project Framework - good for project managers adopting Agile.

## CAPM (Certified Associate in PM)

### Overview
The **CAPM** is PMI's entry-level certification for those with less experience.

### Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Education** | High school or equivalent |
| **PM Education** | 23 hours of PM education |
| **Experience** | Not required |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

### Exam Details

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Format** | 150 questions |
| **Duration** | 180 minutes |
| **Cost** | $300 (PMI member) / $225 (member) |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

**Good stepping stone** to PMP for those building experience.

---

## APM PMQ

### Overview
The **APM Project Management Qualification** is widely recognised in the UK and covers broad PM competencies.

### Details

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Issuing body** | Association for Project Management |
| **Format** | 10 questions, written answers |
| **Duration** | 3 hours |
| **Prerequisites** | None (training recommended) |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

Covers the APM Body of Knowledge - comprehensive UK-focused credential.

## Preparation Tips

### General Advice

1. **Understand the exam format** - Know what to expect
2. **Study the official guide** - PMBOK, PRINCE2 manual, etc.
3. **Take a course** - Structured learning helps (required for some)
4. **Practice exams** - Familiarise yourself with question styles
5. **Join study groups** - Learn with others
6. **Apply concepts** - Relate to real project experience

### Study Resources

| Resource Type | Examples |
|---------------|----------|
| **Official guides** | PMBOK Guide, PRINCE2 Manual |
| **Training courses** | Classroom, virtual, self-paced |
| **Practice exams** | Official and third-party |
| **Study guides** | Rita Mulcahy, Head First PMP |
| **Flashcards** | Key terms and concepts |
| **Forums** | PM communities, Reddit |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}
