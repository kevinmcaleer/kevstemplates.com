# Benefits Review

Benefits review assesses whether the project has delivered the expected benefits and value outlined in the business case.

---

## Purpose

Benefits review ensures:
- Benefits are tracked and measured
- Value delivered is understood
- Lessons are learned for future projects
- Accountability for outcomes
- Business case is validated

## When to Review Benefits

| Timing | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **During project** | Track progress toward benefits |
| **At closure** | Initial assessment of delivery |
| **Post-implementation (3-6 months)** | Early benefits realisation |
| **Post-implementation (12+ months)** | Full benefits realisation |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

---

## Types of Benefits

| Type | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Financial** | Monetary value | Cost savings, revenue increase |
| **Efficiency** | Productivity gains | Time saved, faster processing |
| **Quality** | Improved outcomes | Fewer errors, better service |
| **Compliance** | Meeting requirements | Regulatory compliance |
| **Strategic** | Enabling capability | Market positioning |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

---

## Benefits Review Process

| Step | Activities |
|------|------------|
| **1. Gather data** | Collect metrics and evidence |
| **2. Compare to baseline** | Measure against starting point |
| **3. Assess achievement** | Determine % of benefits realised |
| **4. Analyse variance** | Understand gaps or overachievement |
| **5. Document lessons** | Capture what worked and what didn't |
| **6. Report outcomes** | Communicate to stakeholders |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

---

## Measuring Benefits

| Benefit Type | Measurement Approach |
|--------------|---------------------|
| **Cost savings** | Compare costs before and after |
| **Time savings** | Measure process duration |
| **Quality improvement** | Track error rates, defects |
| **Customer satisfaction** | Survey scores, NPS |
| **Compliance** | Audit results, certifications |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}

---

## Benefits Realisation Report

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Executive summary** | Key outcomes at a glance |
| **Benefits planned** | What was expected |
| **Benefits achieved** | What was delivered |
| **Variance analysis** | Explanation of differences |
| **Lessons learned** | Insights for future |
| **Recommendations** | Actions to improve realisation |

## Common Challenges

| Challenge | Mitigation |
|-----------|------------|
| No baseline data | Capture baseline before project starts |
| Benefits not measurable | Define SMART benefits upfront |
| Attribution difficult | Isolate project impact from other factors |
| Benefits owner unclear | Assign benefit owners from start |
| Review not done | Schedule reviews in project plan |
{:class="table table-single table-narrow"}
